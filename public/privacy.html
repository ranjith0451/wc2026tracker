Privacy Policy

WC26 Hub (mobile app & website) · Effective 4 July 2026

The short version

We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

What the app does

WC26 Hub displays publicly available football information — fixtures, scores, statistics, rankings, and player data — fetched from our own server, which in turn sources it from licensed sports-data providers.

Data we process

  • Nothing personal. Requests from the app to our server contain only the data needed to answer them (e.g. which match you opened). We do not log identities, and the app sends no device identifiers.
  • On-device cache. Match data is cached on your device so the app works offline. It never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Third parties

Sports data is served through our own proxy. Player photographs, where shown, are loaded from the data provider's media servers. No third-party SDKs (analytics, ads, crash reporting) are embedded in the app.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
